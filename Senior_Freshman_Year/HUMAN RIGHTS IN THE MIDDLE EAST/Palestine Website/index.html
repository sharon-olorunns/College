#End_the_Occupation

In the last 50 years years Israel has continued systematic rights abuses of the people of Palestine. Occupation of the West Bank and Gaza has violated war laws and human rights, resulting in many deaths of thousands of Palestinians and even more in severe pain.

This Zionist occupation territory has continued to grow, pushing the Palestines into smaller and smaller territories, turning the West Bank and Gaza into open air prisons. Israel continues to restrict the access to Hospitals, Airports, and resources.

Our goal is to show you the various violations of human rights and the history behind them. We also paln to show the public what life is like in Palestine to help gain understanding of the people that have been relentlessly attacked.
