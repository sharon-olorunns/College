Right to travel

Airport

Yasser Arafat International Airport (Gaza International Airport) was destroyed in 2001.

Borders closed

The Egyptian border opens briefly for a couple of days every couple of months during the year with short notice beforehand and with tons of restrictions on who can get out, not all people with serious conditions can cross the border, students get delayed from attending their universities for months.

Restricted Access to Places of Worship

Erez border barely ever opens for the Palestinian citizens who need urgent treatments or medication. It never opens for tourists, Palestinians are forbidden from praying in Jerusalem in one of the most important holy destinations for Muslims Al-Aqsa Mosque.

Travel by Sea

Travel by the sea is also absolutely fully closed, Palestinians literally get access to only 11km of the sea, which is barely enough for fishing only, bare in mind a greater number of fish exists between 17-22km.

Construction Work

Entry for cement and construction materials is extremely restricted, making reconstruction after all the damage done during wars nearly impossible.
